Melatonin is a rhythm game about dreams

Explore dreams and stay on beat. Melatonin is about the relationship between the dreams we have when we're asleep and the experiences we go through when we're awake.

Half Asleep

Melatonin is being developed in Vancouver by David Huynh who works under the studio name Half Asleep. He previously worked in graphic design, interface design, and user experience design before making the jump to indie game development. This will be the first game released from the developer but there are big plans to build up a team and make more dreamy games after the release of Melatonin.
